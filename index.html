About Dunamai Church

Our Story, Vision & Mission

Our Story

Dunamai Church was founded in 2018 with a vision to To Equip Men for a Holistic Life. What started as a small gathering of 7 believers has grown into a vibrant community of thousands, united in faith and purpose.

Our Vision

To Have men rooted In the word - a place where the broken find healing, the lost discover purpose, and believers are equipped to impact their world for Christ.

Our Mission

We exist to glorify God by making disciples who love Jesus, love people, and transform communities through the power of the Gospel.

Core Values

  • Biblical Truth: We believe in the authority and sufficiency of Scripture
  • Authentic Community: We foster genuine relationships and fellowship
  • Passionate Worship: We worship God with all our hearts
  • Sacrificial Service: We serve others as Christ served us
  • Global Impact: We reach beyond our walls to impact the world
